"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel that follows the journey of a young shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding a worldly treasure. His quest takes him from his homeland in Spain to the Egyptian desert, where he meets a series of characters who guide him toward his personal legend. The novel emphasizes the importance of listening to one's heart, recognizing opportunities, and following dreams. Overall: 4.5 stars from 5.
