Ego Is The Enemy Review

Ego Is The Enemy

"Ego Is the Enemy" by Ryan Holiday is a thought-provoking exploration of how our own ego can be the greatest obstacle to our success, personal growth, and fulfillment. The book draws on a wide range of historical examples and personal anecdotes to illustrate how unchecked ego can lead to arrogance, failure, and missed opportunities. Holiday offers practical advice and timeless wisdom on how to cultivate humility, discipline, and resilience to overcome the destructive force of ego and achieve true greatness. Overall: 4 stars from 5.
