These are the books that I read recently. Here are my reviews on them.

Ego Is The Enemy

"Ego Is the Enemy" by Ryan Holiday is a thought-provoking exploration of how our own ego can be the greatest obstacle to our success, personal growth, and fulfillment

The Alchemist

"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho is a philosophical novel that follows the journey of a young shepherd named Santiago who dreams of finding a worldly treasure.

48 Laws Of Power

"The 48 Laws of Power" by Robert Greene is a guide to understanding and mastering the dynamics of power.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert T. Kiyosaki is a personal finance book that contrasts the financial philosophies of the author's two father figures.

Atomic Habits

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear is a comprehensive guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones.
